About Me

I am currently working as a Quantitative Strategist at Goldman Sachs with main focus in leveraging data and machine learning for equity research.

Previously, I finished my Ph.D. Candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology. During my doctoral research, I worked with Dr. Raghupathy Sivakumar at the intersection of Brain-Computer Interfaces, and Human-Assisted Machine Learning.

I finished my Bachelors from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur in May 2014.

With the mindset of creating a difference in tech industry, I spend my leisure time reading articles on futuristic technology, contemplating over the lag between current state-of-the-art techniques and next generation technological requirements.
